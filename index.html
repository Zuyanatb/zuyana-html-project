My Favourite Flower

The Cherry Blossom

Cherry blossoms are one of my favourite flowers because they are beautiful and have a special meaning in many cultures. They bloom for only a short time each year, which makes them even more precious.
They makes parks and streets look Magical.

What Are Cherry Blossoms?

Let's talk about this
  • They are usually light pink or white
  • They bloom in April, mainly from March to April.
  • The flowers last for only one to two Weeks before falling.

Interesting facts

  1. People celebrate the blooming season with picnics
  2. Some Cherry trees live for hundreds of years.
  3. Cherry blossoms are considered a symbol of hope, renewal, and new beginnings.
